inter•punct

Editor-in-Chief

Inter·punct is a journal for architecture founded at Carnegie Mellon University in 2011.

The inaugural issue, para·meter was published in July 2013.

Inter·punct is a vehicle for the production and dissemination of ideas, positions, and polemics on architecture. The journal provides a space for disputation and debate in order to elicit new forms of discussion and discourse on contemporary architecture and theory.
